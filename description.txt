This is a port of version 1.1.7 of the Calamity Mod, now playable on the modern version of tModLoader! This update released on February 2, 2017 and was important to the mod's history as it added six bosses and the mod's first biome.

Suggestions and bug reports will probably not be taken as to keep the version as true as possible, unless it's to fix an error/inconsistency that occurred during porting.
