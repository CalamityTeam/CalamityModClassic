This is a port of version 1.2 of the Calamity Mod, now playable on the modern version of tModLoader! Major highlights of this update consist of Polterghast and the Stress mechanic, but earlier revisions of the Astral Infection and several bosses are also present.

Suggestions and bug reports will probably not be taken as to keep the version as true as possible, unless it's to fix an error/inconsistency that occurred during porting.
